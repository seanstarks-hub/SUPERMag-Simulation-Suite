# Usadel Equations

The Usadel equation is the quasiclassical transport equation in the diffusive limit,
applicable when the elastic mean free path is much shorter than the coherence length.
It describes the spatial dependence of the anomalous Green's function.

## Key Equations

**Usadel equation:**
$$D \frac{\partial^2 \theta}{\partial x^2} + 2i\omega_n \sin\theta = 0$$

**Self-consistency condition:**
$$\Delta(x) = \lambda \pi T \sum_{\omega_n > 0}^{\omega_D} \sin\theta(\omega_n, x)$$

**Kupriyanov-Lukichev boundary condition at S/F interface:**
$$\gamma_B \xi_S \left.\frac{\partial \theta_S}{\partial x}\right|_{\text{int}} = \sin(\theta_S - \theta_F)$$
