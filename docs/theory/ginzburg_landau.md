# Ginzburg-Landau Free Energy Functional

The Ginzburg-Landau (GL) theory describes superconductivity near Tc through a
complex order parameter ψ and a free energy functional. It captures vortex
states, mixed-state configurations, and domain structures.

## Key Equations

**GL free energy density:**
$$f = \alpha |\psi|^2 + \frac{\beta}{2}|\psi|^4 + \frac{1}{2m^*}\left|(-i\hbar\nabla - 2e\mathbf{A})\psi\right|^2 + \frac{B^2}{2\mu_0}$$

**GL equations:**
$$\alpha\psi + \beta|\psi|^2\psi + \frac{1}{2m^*}(-i\hbar\nabla - 2e\mathbf{A})^2\psi = 0$$
