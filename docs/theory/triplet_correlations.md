# Long-Range Spin-Triplet Correlations

In structures with inhomogeneous magnetization, spin-singlet Cooper pairs can be
converted to spin-triplet pairs. These triplet correlations are immune to pair-breaking
by the exchange field and can propagate over long distances in ferromagnets.

## Key Equations

**Triplet generation at spin-active interface:**
The equal-spin triplet component $f_\uparrow$ is generated at interfaces where
the magnetization direction rotates:

$$f_{\uparrow\uparrow}(x) \propto \sin(\alpha) \exp(-x/\xi_N)$$

where $\alpha$ is the misalignment angle and $\xi_N = \sqrt{\hbar D / 2\pi k_B T}$.
